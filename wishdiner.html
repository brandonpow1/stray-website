WISH DINER

Info

Wish Diner is an upcoming furry visual novel, a side project mainly worked on by two members of the Stray Project, NekoPanda and Rakki. It has themes of wish-granting in similar vein as "The Monkey's Paw" (however a monkey is not expected to appear in this one). Work on the project is currently in the script-writing phase and more info will be provided as it becomes available.

This game is a work of fiction. The story, along with all names, characters and events are fictitious and any resemblance to real-life counterparts (if any) are purely coincidental.
